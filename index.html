Asia Book of Records

At ‘Asia Book of Records’, we believe working with you to unlock the hidden, untapped genius within you to open the world of new possibilities. Asia Book of Records is registered with the Government of India with RNI no HARENG/2011/40693

It’s a platform where the record holders of all major National ‘Book of Records’ including ‘India Book of Records’, ‘Vietnam Book of Records’, ‘Indo-China Book of Records’, ‘Laos Book of Records’ and ‘Nepal Book of Records’ meet to compare, compete and claim the title of ‘Asia Book of Records’ Holder. Today ‘Asia Book of Records’ is having a strong database of 40,000 entries.
